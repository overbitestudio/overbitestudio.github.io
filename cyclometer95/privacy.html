Privacy Policy — Cyclometer 95

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Cyclometer 95 does not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. Your cycling workout history (from Apple Health) and your app data (ride log, trip, and maintenance records) stay on your device and in your own private iCloud account, synced only between your own devices via Apple's CloudKit. The developer never sees, stores, or has access to any of it. There is no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party data sharing of any kind.
